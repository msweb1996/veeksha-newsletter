Bathymetry

While we humans, have embarked upon understanding what lies out there in the Universe, we know very little about the place we inhabit. Presumably, Earth is the only planet known to have water on its surface. But what lies beneath the deep water bodies, their depth or the nature of the surface which holds it is unknown. Only a mere 5% of the oceans have been explored, the remaining 95% vastly remains unknown. We have a better understanding of the surface of Moon or even the Saturn rings than our very own ocean floors. Here is where the need arises in understanding the ocean floor and mapping it.
